Quest Unlock Details

Full quest descriptions for each fusion with a quest path.

FusionQuest GiverZoneQuest Description
Dragon's TeethSir BrunorAshlands (deep)"The Bone Fields are overrun. Something is raising the dead faster than they fall. End it." Clear a necromantic node guarded by endless skeleton waves.
Yggdrasil's RootDame RagnelleWyldwood HearthBreak her curse by planting a Sap seed in enchanted ground and defending it as it grows. She describes the fusion as her "true memory."
Indra's ArrowSir DinadanDrowned Valley"There's a canyon full of iron pillars. Lightning jumps between them. Something lives in there and it's ANGRY." Navigate lightning puzzle, defeat Storm Caller alpha.
Event HorizonLady of the LakeDrowned Valley (deep)Nimue speaks of a "wound in the world" — a void tear consuming everything. Reach it. Stabilize it. "You've seen the void. Now wield it."
DragonbreathSir YvainWhispering SwampHis lion is dying from Fae frostbite. Track a fire-drake and harvest its flame gland. The drake IS the fusion preview.
Rift TideSir BorsFractured CathedralA holy well consumed by void corruption. Water moves in warped sine patterns. Purify by destroying void nodes. "The tide between worlds."
Death's ScytheKing Pellinore (ghost)Swamp depths"My bloodline hunts what cannot be caught. Prove you are worthy." Gauntlet in bone-walled arena with endless bouncing projectiles.
Gáe BolgSir FeirefizCathedral outskirtsIf spared in Level 4: "A spear that freezes fate." Partial recipe + location where time stutters around lightning impacts. Survive time-distortion enemies.
Gebel's SwarmSir DagonetWyldwood"I was hiding in a bell tower and these BAT THINGS went INTO the creatures—" The bell tower is real. Fight the dark bat swarm.
Surtr's CrownSir BrunorIron March border"A fire giant stalks the borderlands. It wears a crown of growing flame. Three knights tried. None returned." Solo boss — defeat it before the ring fills the arena.
Grave CallSir DinadanAshlands"There's a graveyard where the dead bury themselves, and when you step on the graves, they EXPLODE upward. I am not making this up."
Tiamat's GardenHenwen the SowWyldwood (hidden)Follow the magical pig through a trail to a hidden garden. A Sap creature guards it. Recipe inscribed on a living leaf.
Chronos' ChainThe Green KnightWyldwood borderTimed quest from Act I resolves in Act III. Time literally frozen at the crossroads. Fight the Green Knight in frozen space. "You understand patience. Now understand eternity."
Pillars of MuspelheimSir GarethAshlandsDefend Gareth's supply cache from Cinder-type siege waves. Recipe found in scorched journal on enemy corpse.
VajraSir DinadanDrowned Valley"I threw a rock at something electrical and it SHATTERED INTO LIGHTNING. I'm done." The mineral deposit is real. Shatter it for the recipe.
Morrigan's CourtSir DagonetWyldwood (deep)"I watched dark spirits circle a throne for three hours. Too scared to leave. Actually kind of beautiful." The cave and throne are real.
CocytusLady of the LakeDrowned Valley (frozen)"The ice remembers what the water forgets." Frozen Fae battlefield with orbiting ice shards. Navigate, defeat frozen guardian, claim recipe from center.
Boreas' GaleSir YvainWhispering SwampA tornado-like Fae entity destroying the swamp. Yvain's lion tracked it. Defeat it before it reaches inhabited area.
Solomon's SealSir BorsCathedral (crypt)Ancient binding circle in crypt — summons skeletal guardians when triggered. Purify the corrupted seal.
Mjölnir's MarkSir DinadanIron March"Someone left lightning landmines in a FIELD. Who DOES that?" Clear the minefield. Pulse artifact at center holds recipe.
BrynhildrSir BrunorWyldwood Hearth (deep)"A Valkyrie sleeps in a barrow. Her bone swords orbit endlessly, freezing anything near." Hardest combat puzzle in the game.
Fomorian's ChargeThe Green Knight (choice)Wyldwood borderAlt reward during crossroads challenge. Combat trial: become a living shadow projectile. Choose this OR Chronos' Chain.
RagnarökSir Brunor—"A fire giant's heart still beats inside a dead colossus." Reach the interior, destroy the pulsing heart.
RhongomyniadSir KayCamelot gate"The King's spear-technique. He can't wield it anymore. Someone should." Combat trial. Hardest single encounter in the game.
Morrígan's FlockSir Lamorak (ghost)Drowned ValleyAvenge Lamorak's murder. His killer's phantom uses this Fusion against you. Survive it, learn it.
Mistletoe's ChainsSir PelleasWhispering SwampAfter breaking his enchantment: "The Lady's garden had vines of lightning. They healed the earth." Find the garden.
Arondight's ShadowSir TristanIron MarchTristan tells of Lancelot's corrupted blade: "His blade left shadows." Find Lancelot's abandoned camp.
Niflheim BlastSir DagonetWyldwood"I sneezed in a bone cave and everything FROZE and then SHATTERED and then MORE BONES and I ran." The cave is real.
Green Man's EmbraceBrother CaradocFractured CathedralThe monk's garden is corrupted — plants possess wandering animals. Help purify it. Learn to control the effect.
Tesla's VolleySir SafirEastern ruinsPalamedes' brother brings a message: "My brother says see the lightning gallery." Natural bouncing-lightning phenomenon.
Excalibur's LightParzival (finale)Twilight ConfluenceThe ONLY story-rewarded Fusion. Parzival speaks to Morien for the first and only time: "Take this. For what you've given." Post-game only.
Arachne's LoomDame Ragnelle (post-curse)Wyldwood HearthIn true form, Ragnelle teaches Fae weaving: "The old magic moves in waves. When it breaks, it catches." Combat trial.
Camelot's WardSir GawainIron March bridgeGawain at the bridge: "I've held this position for nine days. Want to know how? Watch." Survive his wave.
Mordred's TrapSir KayCamelotKay finds the recipe in Mordred's abandoned quarters. "The traitor left his notes." Uncomfortable to wield — it's Mordred's technique.
